AI Max Box Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

- Account information: Email, password, username

- Billing information: Payment methods, transaction history

- Usage information: Services accessed, queries made, settings

- Device information: IP address, browser type, operating system

2. How We Use Information

- Provide requested services like AI chat and image generation

- Process payments and prevent fraud

- Improve and develop new product features

- Customize content and optimize user experience

- Security and debugging

How We Share Information

-Only share user information with third party service providers to operate our services, such as payment processors, cloud hosting providers etc.

-Require all third parties to comply with strict privacy and security standards

- Do not share user data with third parties for their own purposes

4. Data Storage and Protection

- Store user account data on secure cloud servers

-Implement encryption, firewalls, access controls to protect dataImplement encryption, firewalls, access controls to protect data0

- Retain data as long as user account is active. Allow users to delete account and data.

-Anonymize usage analytics data before storage.

5. Children's Privacy

- Do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16.

-For users under 18, consent from a parent or guardian is required.

6. Comply with GDPR, CCPA and other data regulations

- Allow users to access, export and delete their data

-Require opt-in consent for optional data collection

-Notify users of any breach or unauthorized access

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

- Notify users of any changes to this policy through app updates or notifications
